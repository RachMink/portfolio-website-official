About

Sitdown was created focused on a user's needs and experience. Starting with contextual inquiry, storyboarding, wireframing, producing mock-ups and prototypes and finally coding the user interface, this app was developed to make collaboratively planning an event easier. Users of the app can create events, invite other users, assign items to be brought and manage events they're invited to with ease.
